PERSONAL WORK

JUST FOR FUN LIFESTYLE PHOTOGRAPHY

My favorite way to shoot is to just pick up the model and drive around aimlessly. We’d just hop out the car and shoot if we see something interesting, then move on and search for the next location. The lack of structure with the element of surprise made the whole experience a lot more exciting and unpredictable.
